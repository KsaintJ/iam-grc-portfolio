About

The Story

I founded Juste™ LLC in March 2026 and built Least by Juste™ — a live SaaS product that automates Google Workspace access reviews and generates SOC 2 CC6.x-aligned audit evidence for SMBs. Every access governance framework documented in this portfolio is something I also implemented in production code.

Before founding Juste™ LLC, I spent five years at TeamViewer as SDR → Inside Sales → Customer Support Specialist — working daily with enterprise IT teams on SSO/SAML configurations, Conditional Access policies, MFA enforcement, provisioning and deprovisioning workflows, and SOC 2 compliance inquiries. I saw governance problems disguised as technical tickets every single day. That's what made me build Least by Juste™.

My B.S. in Information Science coursework in CIS 4365 (Security Policies, NIST CSF), CIS 4361 (Information Security Management, CISSP-aligned), and CIS 4204 (Ethical Hacking) — plus a 3.76 GPA — gave me the framework foundation. Twelve years of professional experience gave me the operational context. Least by Juste™ is where both came together.

Currently pursuing CompTIA Security+ SY0-701 (target September 2026) and SC-300 next. Actively seeking GRC Analyst, Information Security Analyst, and Solutions Engineer roles in Tampa Bay and remote.
